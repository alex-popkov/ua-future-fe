Help UA Future Charity
to make miracles come true

We have been supporting Ukraine since 2014 by providing humanitarian aid directly to the people living closest to and located in the war zones.

Together with our international partners, we deliver food, medical equipment, and a wide variety of other humanitarian help to civilians affected by the war and to battlefield hospitals. We also support orphanages and women’s hospitals near the front line. Our goal is to provide this necessary assistance directly.
